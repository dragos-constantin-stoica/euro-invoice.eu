Get stared in 3 easy steps

Joining Euro Invoice takes less than 5 minutes and you can do it in 3 easy steps:

  • 01 Go to the homepage of the application.

    https://euro-invoice.eu/app

  • 02 Click on Register NEW Company

    In order to create you Company account, click on Registre NEW Company.

  • 03 Fill in the registration form and you are DONE!

    You have to fill in the registation form. The email address that you provide will be your login too. You need to provide: Your Company full name, National Registration Number of your Company, The country that your Company is registered in.

 

Finalize your Company setup

Once your Company is enrolled in Euro Invoice you will be redirected to the login page. Use your email and password to login. In order to finalize the full setup you need to provide some operational information about your Company: V.A.T number, contact mobile number, contact email address, Company official address, Company bank account, Select invoice serial number format.. This information is used on the invoice and is mandatory.
You need to create at least one Product/Service. Then create your 1st Client and your 1st Contract.

Congratulations !!! You are ready now to create your 1st invoice!

You can manage the invoice lifecycle in Payments section. The invoice status may be: New, Overdue, Partially payed, and Payed. The financial situation is immediately available on Dashboard. Here you can monitor the invoice status, the payments for the current year, and multiannual payments since the beggining of the registation in Euro Invoice.

Frequently Asked Questions

We prepared a series of answers for you and we will be more than happy to provide more information via the contact form.

  • What does it mean Always Free?

    It means free of use of Euro Invoice without any charges, now or in the future. We are commited to help and support freelancers and small businesses, with a volume of 1 to 3 invoices per month. We strongly believe that small businesses are the back bone of the economy and we should help and encourage them to function.

  • How do I have access to my Company data?

    All your Company data is strored separately by design. If you decide that you need to get all your Company data, this will be possible upon request.

  • How do I get support?

    All plans have support included. The Free plan has standard support via contact form on this page, the Business plan has dedicated support via the Euro Invoice support section, and the Enterprise plan has dedicated support via our support team, with the possibility to have onsite support on demand.

  • What is EPC QR code payment?

    Accoding to Wikipedia: "The European Payments Council Quick Response Code guidelines define the content of a QR code that can be used to initiate SEPA credit transfer (SCT). It contains all the necessary information in clear text. These QR code guidelines are used on many invoices and payment requests in the countries that support it (Austria, Belgium, Finland, Germany, The Netherlands) enabling tens of millions to pay without requiring manual input leading to lower error rates.".
    Short answer: it is a QR code that allows to make quick eInvoice SEPA payments in certain EU countries. You can pay the invoice using the camera of your smart phone and a specific SEPA payment application. See the offical EU sites for full information.
